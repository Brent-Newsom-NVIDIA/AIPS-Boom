## Summary
CoolerX is a retail ISV focused on turning cooler and freezer surfaces into IoT-enabled smart screens. NVIDIA's retail partner page positions CoolerX for in-store media, merchandising, consumer engagement, and traffic monetization.

## Detail
Use CoolerX when the workload is retail media, in-store merchandising, cooler/freezer aisle engagement, or connected data-driven shopper messaging. The featured implementation uses AI and data science over first-party connected data such as promotions, prices, point-of-sale signals, and weather to influence purchase decisions.

The NVIDIA technologies listed are NVIDIA T4, NVIDIA A30, CUDA, TensorFlow, and NVIDIA Docker. The results section emphasizes more relevant content, increased growth and revenue, and improved traffic monetization.

[[CoolerX-Kroger-NVIDIA-In-Store-Retail-Media]] adds named retailer proof for Kroger and public NVIDIA collaboration evidence. NVIDIA's GTC 2024 catalog includes a Cooler Screens/NVIDIA session focused on transforming Kroger stores, and CoolerX public material says the software was developed in collaboration with NVIDIA and Microsoft and was operating in retailers including Kroger, Walgreens, Giant Eagle's GetGo, Chevron, and Western Union.
