## Summary
Centific is a retail ISV featured by NVIDIA for intelligent-store operations through the Pitaya Retail Intelligence Platform. It combines computer vision and generative AI for loss prevention, inventory, safety, workforce efficiency, and operational compliance.

## Detail
Use Centific when a customer wants a broader store-operations platform rather than a single video analytics point solution. NVIDIA's retail partner page says Centific supports integrations with existing systems of record plus computer vision and generative AI training, inferencing, and maintenance.

The highlighted NVIDIA technologies are [[NVIDIA-Metropolis]], [[NVIDIA-NIM]], [[NeMo-Platform]], and NVIDIA GPUs such as A2 and L4. The featured results include protecting the bottom line from shrinkage, capturing lost sales from out-of-stock items, improving workforce efficiency through task management, and increasing compliance with standard operating procedures.

[[Centific-NVIDIA-Edge-Retail-Loss-Prevention]] adds strong anonymized deployment proof for a leading home improvement retailer using Centific computer vision and NVIDIA edge computing at checkout lanes. The customer is not named, but the public source reports 175 participating stores, 180M in annual shrinkage reduction, and 75 labor hours saved per week.
