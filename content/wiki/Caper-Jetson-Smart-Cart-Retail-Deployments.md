## Summary
Caper Jetson Smart Cart Retail Deployments is a named-retailer proof point for Caper by Instacart smart carts powered by NVIDIA Jetson. Instacart's source names multiple retailers offering Caper Carts and describes the cart's AI, computer vision, integrated scale, and personalization capabilities.

## Detail
Instacart says Caper Carts are powered by [[NVIDIA-Jetson-Platform]] technology. The source describes Caper Cart as a smart cart with an integrated scale, cameras, barcode recognition, shopping-list integration, gamification, and location-based features that support in-store product discovery, checkout assistance, and retail media.

Named retailers in the source include [[Bristol-Farms]], [[Fairway-Market]], [[The-Fresh-Grocer]], [[Geisslers-Supermarket]], [[Kroger]], [[McKeevers-Market-and-Eatery]], [[Price-Chopper]], [[Schnucks]], [[ShopRite]], and [[ALDI]]. The article also says Schnucks is scaling Caper Carts to most of its stores after shoppers gave strong feedback.
