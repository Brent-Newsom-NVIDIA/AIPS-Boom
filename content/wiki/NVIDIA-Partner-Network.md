## Summary
NVIDIA Partner Network is NVIDIA's partner ecosystem program for organizations that build, sell, deploy, or integrate NVIDIA-based solutions. In the retail wiki context, it matters because the NVIDIA retail partner page marks some ISVs as NPN Preferred Partners and uses partner categories to help customers find implementation support.

## Detail

It is not a substitute for the specific ISV or OEM pages; instead, it explains the partner-program context that links retail ISVs, solution providers, cloud providers, system builders, and Enterprise RA endorsed OEM designs.
