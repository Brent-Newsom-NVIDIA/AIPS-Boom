## Summary
Avathon has public NVIDIA VSS integration proof for AI-powered video intelligence. The source does not name a retail customer, so use it as NVIDIA integration evidence for Avathon's video platform rather than as a named-customer case study.

## Detail
Avathon announced an initiative to integrate the NVIDIA Blueprint for Video Search and Summarization into its video intelligence platform. The stated goal is to help industrial and infrastructure organizations search, summarize, and analyze large volumes of video using natural language, improving safety, situational awareness, and operational efficiency.

This proof point is strongest for video intelligence, safety, incident investigation, HSE, logistics, manufacturing, and large camera-network analysis. In retail recommendations, use it when Avathon is shortlisted for store analytics, queue analytics, stock-out detection, or footfall analysis and the customer also wants natural-language video search or summarization capabilities.

The source explicitly ties Avathon to NVIDIA Metropolis' VSS blueprint and an advanced video AI stack. It does not provide named customer evidence, so any recommendation should say Avathon has NVIDIA VSS integration proof and separately ask whether a customer reference is required.
