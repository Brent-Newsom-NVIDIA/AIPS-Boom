## Summary
LiveX AI GTC Toy Jensen Holographic Agent is a named-deployment proof point for LiveX AI at NVIDIA GTC. NVIDIA's GTC 2026 news blog describes Toy Jensen as a holographic AI assistant built by LiveX.ai using NVIDIA hardware and software including RTX 6000 GPUs, NVIDIA NIM microservices, and the Nemotron language model.

## Detail
The deployment is not a retail customer case study, but it is a strong public NVIDIA deployment proof point for LiveX AI. It matters to the wiki because it demonstrates the same agentic and customer-interaction stack that can be applied to retail concierge, event retail, in-store assistance, and branded digital-human support.

The NVIDIA blog says Toy Jensen answered visitor questions, used a digital twin, and ran on on-premises edge systems with NVIDIA RTX 6000 GPUs, NVIDIA NIM microservices, and [[Nemotron]]. That makes it relevant to [[NVIDIA-NIM]], digital-human workflows, [[NVIDIA-ACE]], [[NVIDIA-Tokkio-Digital-Human-Blueprint]], and edge inference patterns.

For recommendations, use this page as supporting deployment evidence for LiveX AI when the customer wants an embodied or venue-based AI assistant rather than a purely chat-based ecommerce agent. Use [[PUMA-LiveX-AI-Store-Concierge]] as the stronger named retail customer proof point.
