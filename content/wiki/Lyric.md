## Summary
Lyric is a retail and supply-chain ISV featured by NVIDIA for intelligent supply chain workloads. The NVIDIA retail partner page highlights Lyric Studio integrated with NVIDIA cuOpt for routing, network simulation, scenario planning, and store-level cost-to-serve analysis.

## Detail
Use Lyric when the workload is supply-chain optimization, vehicle routing, store replenishment, scenario simulation, cost-to-serve analysis, or moving planning from batch cycles to interactive decision support. NVIDIA's page frames Lyric's platform as composable intelligence that combines data layers, specialized algorithms, no-code workflows, and configurable user experiences.

The named NVIDIA technology is [[cuOpt]]. Featured results include 240x faster routing, a 200-basis-point improvement over CPU alternatives, evaluating 100+ scenarios in the previous time for one, and moving from overnight batch processing to near-real-time decisions.
