## Summary
Lawson China is a named retailer context for SandStar's AI-driven store-operations work. SandStar's AI Store page names Lawson's AI-driven growth strategy as a real-world case and states that SandStar uses NVIDIA AI infrastructure and models for scalable retail AI training and real-time inference.

## Detail
Use this page when the workload is store operations, frontline task support, AI-assisted SOP design, store compliance, loss prevention, merchandising execution, or operational analytics across many physical retail locations. SandStar positions the AI Store platform around role-based assistance, long-duration activity modeling, natural-language store analysis, and real-world retail data.

The SandStar source says the Lawson case focuses on embedding AI into frontline workflows for staff, managers, and regional operations teams, with payback within three to six months. The same source identifies SandStar as an NVIDIA Cloud Partner using NVIDIA AI infrastructure and models for retail AI model training and real-time inference.

This is useful as a recommendation proof point for [[SandStar]] because it ties a named retailer context to store operations and NVIDIA-backed AI infrastructure. Use it alongside [[NVIDIA-Metropolis]], [[NVIDIA-DeepStream]], and [[NVIDIA-Jetson-Platform]] when the customer wants a physical-store AI stack that moves beyond single-camera alerts into operating-model improvement.
