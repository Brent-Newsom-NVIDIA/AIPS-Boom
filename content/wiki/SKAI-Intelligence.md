## Summary
SKAI Intelligence is a retail and CPG ISV focused on industrial-grade synthetic data, high-fidelity digital twins, and scalable 3D content generation. NVIDIA's retail partner page highlights SKAI's use of NVIDIA Isaac Sim, Omniverse, RTX rendering, and USD pipelines for production-grade retail assets.

## Detail
Use SKAI Intelligence when the workload is product digital twins, synthetic data generation, ecommerce 3D assets, marketing content, simulation-ready assets, or AI-generated content pipelines for retail and CPG. The featured solution uses AI-trained robotic 3D capture and simulation to recreate product geometry, materials, and optics at scale.

The listed NVIDIA technologies are [[NVIDIA-Omniverse]], [[NVIDIA-Isaac-Sim]], NVIDIA RTX rendering, and USD pipeline. Featured results include faster production versus traditional CGI, lower cost through automation and asset reuse, real-time preview, and brand-grade realism across retail/commerce campaigns.
