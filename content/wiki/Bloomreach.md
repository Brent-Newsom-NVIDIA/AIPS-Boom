## Summary
Bloomreach is a retail ISV for omnichannel ecommerce personalization and search. NVIDIA's retail partner page highlights Bloomreach's use of NVIDIA NeMo and NIM embedding microservices to improve ecommerce search relevance and reduce latency.

## Detail
Use Bloomreach when the workload is ecommerce search, product discovery, personalization, or unifying customer and product data across channels. The featured implementation connects Bloomreach's Loomi AI with NVIDIA NIM embeddings to better understand complex search queries and deliver more precise results.

The NVIDIA technologies listed for the featured solution are [[NeMo-Platform]], [[NVIDIA-NIM]], NVIDIA L4, CUDA, and the embed-qa-4 NIM. Pair Bloomreach with [[NVIDIA-AI-Enterprise]] and [[Triton-Inference-Server]] when answering about production deployment of retail search or personalization inference.
