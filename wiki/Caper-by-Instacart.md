## Summary
Caper by Instacart is a retail ISV for AI-powered smart carts. NVIDIA's retail partner page positions Caper Cart for personalized in-store shopping, item recognition, navigation, checkout assistance, and omnichannel engagement powered by NVIDIA Jetson.

## Detail
Use Caper when the customer asks about smart carts, checkout assistance, in-store product discovery, cart-based retail media, or adding intelligence directly to the shopper journey. The featured solution describes four computer-vision cameras, barcode recognition, an integrated scale for produce, gamification, and location-based features.

The NVIDIA technology highlighted is [[NVIDIA-Jetson-Platform]]. The results listed by NVIDIA include incremental consumer spend per use, a 70+ Net Promoter Score, and average screen engagement time of about 30 minutes.
