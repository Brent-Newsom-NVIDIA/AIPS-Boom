## Summary
Zabka is a Polish convenience-store retailer named in NVIDIA's AiFi autonomous retail article. The article identifies Zabka as an AiFi deployment example for autonomous stores tied to NVIDIA GPU-powered edge AI and NVIDIA Metropolis.

## Detail
NVIDIA says Zabka opened its first public autonomous store in Poznan, Poland using AiFi. The article also describes the rollout of Zabka Nano autonomous stores and connects the shopper journey to Zabka's mobile app ecosystem.

For recommendations, use Zabka as a proof point when a customer wants international autonomous convenience-store examples, app-connected checkout-free shopping, or a store format that scales beyond a single lab pilot.
