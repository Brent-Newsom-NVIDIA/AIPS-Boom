## Summary
LiveX AI is a retail ISV for omnichannel customer experience and AI agents. NVIDIA's retail partner page highlights LiveX AI's use of NVIDIA NIM to deliver real-time, human-like chat, voice, and video experiences.

## Detail
Use LiveX AI when a retail workload centers on customer service automation, shopping assistance, product education, proactive support, upsell/renewal workflows, or reducing return/support costs. The NVIDIA page positions the solution as personalized AI agents across multiple platforms.

The highlighted NVIDIA technologies are [[NVIDIA-NIM]], [[NeMo-Platform]], [[Triton-Inference-Server]], and NVIDIA A100. The featured results include a 5x increase in lead conversions, up to 85% support cost reduction, 80% self-resolution, 30%+ churn reduction, and 15% return reduction.

### Named customer and deployment examples

[[PUMA-LiveX-AI-Store-Concierge]] is the strongest named retail customer proof point found in Batch 2. Retail TouchPoints says [[PUMA]] is launching an AI-powered interactive in-store concierge with LiveX AI, and names NVIDIA Nemotron, [[NeMo-Platform]], and [[NVIDIA-NIM]] as part of the experience.

[[LiveX-AI-GTC-Toy-Jensen-Holographic-Agent]] is a strong NVIDIA deployment proof point rather than a retail customer case. NVIDIA's GTC 2026 blog says LiveX.ai built a holographic Toy Jensen assistant using NVIDIA RTX 6000 GPUs, [[NVIDIA-NIM]] microservices, and [[Nemotron]].
