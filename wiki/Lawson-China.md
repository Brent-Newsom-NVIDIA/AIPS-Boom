## Detail
Use Lawson China as supporting customer context for SandStar recommendations, not as a standalone NVIDIA platform page. The relevant workload is AI-assisted store operations: staff, manager, and regional operations support, store-level analysis, operational SOPs, and retail performance improvement.

The source evidence should be read carefully: it names Lawson in a SandStar real-world case and says SandStar uses NVIDIA AI infrastructure and models, but it does not provide a full customer case-study narrative with architecture diagrams. For recommendations, it is still useful because it connects a named retailer, an ISV from the NVIDIA retail ecosystem, and NVIDIA-backed retail AI infrastructure.
