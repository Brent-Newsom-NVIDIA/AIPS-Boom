## Summary
Lenovo appears in this wiki as both an NVIDIA partner for Enterprise Reference Architecture infrastructure and as the edge AI infrastructure provider in the Kroger Everseen checkout-loss-prevention case study. In the Kroger case, Lenovo supplied Edge AI servers powered by NVIDIA GPUs to run Everseen Visual AI for self-checkout analytics.

## Detail
For retail loss prevention, Lenovo's role is infrastructure and deployment enablement rather than the ISV application. The Kroger case study names Lenovo ThinkSystem SR630 with NVIDIA T4 Tensor Core GPUs and Lenovo ThinkSystem SR550 as part of the solution stack, with each edge server able to process up to 20 high-resolution cameras in real time.

For AI factory infrastructure, Lenovo also appears in [[NVIDIA-Enterprise-RA-Partner-Endorsed-Designs]] through the Lenovo Hybrid AI 289 Enterprise RA entry using ThinkSystem systems and NVIDIA HGX platforms.
