## Summary
Instacart is the grocery technology company behind Caper by Instacart and the Caper Cart smart-cart platform. Its Caper Jetson source explicitly ties Caper Carts to NVIDIA Jetson technology and names multiple retailer deployments.

## Detail
Instacart positions Caper Cart as an AI-powered smart cart that improves in-store shopping through item recognition, an integrated scale, product recommendations, shopping-list support, gamification, and location-based features.
