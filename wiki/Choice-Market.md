## Summary
Choice Market is a Denver-based grocery and convenience retailer named in NVIDIA's AiFi autonomous retail article. The source uses Choice Market as a named customer example for AiFi checkout-free shopping powered by NVIDIA GPU-accelerated edge AI and NVIDIA Metropolis.

## Detail
NVIDIA reports that Choice Market adopted AiFi's checkout-free option at a Denver store and saw customers using the option increase sales by about 20 percent. The article also reports repeat usage, with many first-time users returning within a month.

For wiki recommendations, Choice Market is the strongest KPI-style proof point in [[AiFi-Metropolis-Autonomous-Retail-Deployments]]. It is useful when answering customer questions about autonomous checkout ROI, shopper adoption, and convenience-store automation.
