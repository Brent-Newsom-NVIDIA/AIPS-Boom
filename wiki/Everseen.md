## Summary
Everseen is a retail ISV focused on vision AI for loss prevention, inventory management, checkout, stockroom, and customer experience. NVIDIA's retail partner page highlights Evercheck as a checkout loss-prevention solution and positions Everseen for measurable retail ROI. [[Kroger-Everseen-Checkout-Loss-Prevention]] adds a named-customer case study showing Everseen Visual AI running on Lenovo edge servers with NVIDIA T4 Tensor Core GPUs.

## Detail
Use Everseen when the workload is checkout shrink, product-scan validation, aisle/stockroom loss prevention, queue management, or perpetual inventory accuracy. NVIDIA's page names Evercheck, Evershelf, and Everdoor as related solution areas.

The listed NVIDIA technologies include [[TensorRT]], [[Triton-Inference-Server]], CUDA, NVIDIA Docker, NVIDIA PyTorch, NVIDIA T4, NVIDIA L4, NVIDIA A2, and NVIDIA A30. The NVIDIA page cites results from a commissioned Forrester TEI study, including ROI, payback period, per-store benefit, and staff productivity claims.

### Named customer example
[[Kroger-Everseen-Checkout-Loss-Prevention]] describes Kroger using Everseen Visual AI to reduce self-checkout scanning errors, retail shrink, and customer friction. The Lenovo case study says the deployment used Lenovo Edge AI servers with NVIDIA T4 Tensor Core GPUs, processed up to 20 high-resolution checkout cameras per edge server in real time, and had been deployed to 1,700 Kroger grocery stores at the time of publication.
