## Summary
Graymatics is a retail ISV for intelligent-store video analytics. NVIDIA's retail partner page describes Graymatics VistaMart as a video analytics solution for customer demographics, dwell time, foot traffic, inventory levels, asset protection, and store analytics.

## Detail
Use Graymatics when the workload is general in-store video analytics and the customer wants to combine store intelligence with asset protection. The NVIDIA retail partner page positions Graymatics for smarter, more profitable, and more effective retail operations.
