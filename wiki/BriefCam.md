## Summary
BriefCam is a retail ISV focused on video analytics for intelligent stores. NVIDIA's retail partner page positions BriefCam for video metadata search, alerting, visualization, loss prevention, and customer-behavior analytics.

## Detail
Use BriefCam when the customer has existing surveillance infrastructure and wants to convert video into proactive store operations intelligence. The featured retail use case emphasizes shrinkage, theft, vandalism, ROI from existing surveillance, and centralized analytics for shopper behavior and trends.

The NVIDIA page associates BriefCam with NVIDIA Ampere A10, RTX A2000, RTX A4000, RTX 4080 Ada, and NVIDIA Ada L4. For NVIDIA stack recommendations, pair BriefCam with [[NVIDIA-Metropolis]], [[NVIDIA-DeepStream]], and [[NVIDIA-Video-Search-and-Summarization-Blueprint]] when the query is about searching footage, alert verification, summarization, or store incident analytics.

[[BriefCam-NVIDIA-Metropolis-Video-Analytics]] adds NVIDIA-specific integration proof. The strongest retail source is an NVIDIA/Loss Prevention Research Council organized retail crime workflow where BriefCam contributes vehicle license plate and model recognition in a Metropolis-based solution. BriefCam also has non-retail named deployment evidence with Hartford Police Department as an early beneficiary of BriefCam running on NVIDIA GPUs.
