## Summary
SandStar is a retail ISV for intelligent-store and unattended-shopping solutions. NVIDIA's retail partner page highlights SandStar AI Vending, Smart Store, and Unattended Shop, using AI and big data to improve operations and shopping experiences.

## Detail
Use SandStar when the workload is AI vending, smart-store operations, unattended shops, store compliance, merchandising operations, labor scheduling, or semantic action recognition in physical retail. NVIDIA's page says SandStar has implementations in 18 countries and focuses on retail operational efficiency and decision-making.

The named NVIDIA technologies are [[NVIDIA-Jetson-Platform]] and [[NVIDIA-DeepStream]]. Featured results include reduced vending machine costs, higher average transaction value, improved operational decisions, and enhanced store compliance monitoring.

[[Lawson-China-SandStar-NVIDIA-Store-Operations]] adds named retailer context for SandStar's AI Store work. SandStar names Lawson's AI-driven growth strategy as a real-world case and says SandStar uses NVIDIA AI infrastructure and models for scalable retail AI model training and real-time inference.
