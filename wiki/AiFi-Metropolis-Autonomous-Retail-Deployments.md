## Summary
AiFi Metropolis Autonomous Retail Deployments is a named-deployment proof point for checkout-free and autonomous-store workloads using AiFi with NVIDIA technology. NVIDIA's blog ties AiFi to NVIDIA Metropolis and NVIDIA GPU-powered edge AI, and names multiple retail deployments across grocery, convenience, stadium/event, and international store formats.

## Detail
The NVIDIA blog describes AiFi's autonomous shopping platform as using real-time image recognition and edge AI powered by NVIDIA GPUs. It also identifies AiFi as using [[NVIDIA-Metropolis]] for smart video analytics, making this a strong workload proof point for retail computer vision, camera-based shopper journey understanding, and checkout-free store operations.

Named deployments in the source include [[Choice-Market]] in Denver, [[Zabka]] in Poland, a 200-square-foot autonomous store with [[Penske-Entertainment]] and [[Verizon-Business]] at the Indianapolis 500, [[Loop-Neighborhood]] stores in California, Carrefour's Flash LabStore, and a Morrisons checkout-free test store in the U.K. The strongest KPI-style evidence in the article is the Choice Market example, where customers using the checkout-free option reportedly increased sales at the Denver store by about 20 percent and showed repeat-use behavior.

Use this page when a customer asks about autonomous stores, frictionless checkout, stadium/event retail, convenience-store automation, or camera-led intelligent-store analytics. Recommended NVIDIA stack context is [[NVIDIA-Metropolis]] plus [[NVIDIA-DeepStream]] and edge GPU deployment, with [[AiFi]] as the ISV implementation layer.
