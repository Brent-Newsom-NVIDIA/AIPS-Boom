## Summary
PUMA LiveX AI Store Concierge is a named-customer proof point for LiveX AI in retail customer experience. The Retail TouchPoints article says PUMA is launching an AI shopping assistant with LiveX AI and Google Cloud, using NVIDIA Nemotron, NeMo, and NIM to support in-store and mobile shopping experiences.

## Detail
The source describes PUMA's AI concierge as an interactive assistant available through digital displays and shoppers' mobile devices. The workload maps to omnichannel retail agents, product discovery, personalized assistance, loyalty engagement, and interactive in-store experiences.

The NVIDIA tie is explicit: the article says the experience uses NVIDIA Nemotron, [[NeMo-Platform]], and [[NVIDIA-NIM]] to support the AI assistant.
