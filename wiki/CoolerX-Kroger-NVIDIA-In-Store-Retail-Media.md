## Summary
CoolerX provides a named retail-media proof point for Kroger and other large retailers using AI-driven smart screens in stores. NVIDIA's GTC 2024 on-demand catalog includes a session titled around Cooler Screens and NVIDIA transforming Kroger stores, while CoolerX public material says the software was developed in collaboration with NVIDIA and Microsoft.

## Detail
Use this page when the workload is in-store retail media, smart cooler/freezer surfaces, digital merchandising, or shopper engagement rather than shrink prevention. CoolerX uses AI and data science to identify marketing activation opportunities inside physical stores, operate smart screens, and adapt content based on contextual and first-party retail signals.

The public CoolerX launch release says the software was developed in collaboration with Microsoft and NVIDIA, and that it was already operating in top retailers including Kroger, Walgreens, Giant Eagle's GetGo, Chevron, and Western Union. The NVIDIA GTC session gives a stronger NVIDIA-specific named context by explicitly tying Cooler Screens, NVIDIA, and Kroger stores together.

For stack recommendations, pair CoolerX with edge AI infrastructure, in-store data integration, and adjacent [[NVIDIA-Metropolis]] or [[NVIDIA-DeepStream]] analytics patterns if the customer wants physical-store media surfaces that react to shelf availability, browsing, dwell, attention, and merchandising context.
