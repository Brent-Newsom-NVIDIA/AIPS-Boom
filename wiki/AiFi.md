## Summary
AiFi is a retail ISV in NVIDIA's Independent Software Vendor partner set for intelligent stores. It focuses on autonomous shopping and spatial intelligence using camera-led computer vision that can adapt to existing store formats without shelf sensors.

## Detail
AiFi is best matched to workloads involving autonomous shopping, shopper journey tracking, physical-space analytics, and store process automation. NVIDIA's retail partner page highlights AiFi's ability to support multiple entry and checkout journeys, including app, credit card, gated, and hybrid entry models.

For recommendations, position AiFi with [[NVIDIA-Metropolis]], [[NVIDIA-DeepStream]], and edge/data-center GPU deployment when a customer wants spatial understanding across people and objects rather than a single checkout or shelf application. The NVIDIA page highlights NVIDIA T4, NVIDIA A6000, and CUDA as technologies associated with the featured AiFi solution.
